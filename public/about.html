Liam's Call

About Us

Our mission

Liam's Call (liamscall.com) exists for one reason: to make sure no caregiver or family member facing a mental health, addiction, or housing challenge has to face it alone. We believe that accessible, judgment-free support — available at any hour, without a waitlist or a co-pay — can make a real difference in people's lives.

We built Liam's Call as a safe starting point: a free AI chat where you can put words to what you are feeling, find crisis resources in one place, and take a breath before deciding what to do next. We are not a sales or phone receptionist product, and we are not a marketing or email platform — we focus only on caregiver and family wellbeing.

Who we are

Liam's Call is a Canadian mental health technology project focused on caregiver and family wellbeing. We are a small team with a deep personal connection to the challenges of caregiving — many of us have navigated the mental and emotional weight of supporting a loved one through illness, aging, or crisis.

How the AI works

Liam's Call uses a large language model (AI) to have supportive, open-ended conversations. The AI is specifically guided to respond with warmth and care to caregiving challenges, emotional distress, and questions about mental health resources.

Important: Liam's Call is a support tool, not a clinical service. The AI is not a therapist, counsellor, or medical professional. It does not diagnose conditions, prescribe treatment, or replace professional mental health care. If you or someone you know is in crisis, please use the resources on our Resources page or call 9-8-8 immediately.

Our commitment to safety

  • The AI is trained to recognize crisis language and always direct users to emergency services and crisis lines first.
  • We do not store your chat messages beyond your session. Conversations are not used to train AI models.
  • We do not sell, share, or monetize your personal data.
  • We continuously review AI responses and update our safety guidelines as standards evolve.
  • If the AI ever gives you a response that feels harmful or inappropriate, please report it to hello@liamscall.com — your feedback directly improves the tool.

Frequently asked questions

What is Liam's Call?

Liam's Call is a Canadian mental health technology project offering free AI chat support for caregivers and families facing mental health, addiction, and housing challenges.

Who is Liam's Call for?

Caregivers, family members, and anyone seeking next steps around mental health, addiction, or housing support in Canada and the U.S.

Is Liam's Call free?

Yes. The chat is free to use with no account, waitlist, or co-pay.
