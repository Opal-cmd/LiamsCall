About Us

Our mission

Liam's Call (liamscall.com) exists for one reason: to make sure no caregiver or family member facing a mental health, addiction, or housing challenge has to face it alone. We believe that accessible, judgment-free support — available at any hour, without a waitlist or a co-pay — can make a real difference in people's lives.

We built Liam's Call as a safe starting point: a free AI web chat where you can put words to what you are feeling, find crisis resources, and take a breath before deciding what to do next. We are not a click-to-call widget company, not an AI phone receptionist — we focus on caregiver and family wellbeing. The chat localizes support to your city and country wherever you are.

Who we are

Liam's Call is a mental health technology project focused on caregiver and family wellbeing. We are a small team with a deep personal connection to the challenges of caregiving for family members and loved ones dealing with chronic, co-existent mental health, addiction and housing issues.

Category: Health Sciences, Social/Civic Services — Mental Health, Addiction, Homelessness.

We help caregivers and families find next steps through a localized chat, and we publish verified national directories on our Resources page rather than outdated clinic phone lists.

How the AI works

Liam's Call uses language-based AI with a control layer to have supportive, open-ended conversations. The AI is specifically guided to respond with warmth and care to caregiving challenges, emotional distress, and questions about mental health, addiction and housing support resources. It is multilingual and can detect and engage in your preferred native language/s.

Important: Liam's Call is a support tool, not a clinical service. The AI is not a therapist, counsellor, or medical professional. It does not diagnose conditions, prescribe treatment, or replace professional mental health care. If you or someone you know is in crisis, please use the resources on our Resources page or call 9-8-8 immediately.

Our commitment to safety

  • The AI is trained to recognize crisis language and always direct users to emergency services and crisis lines first.
  • We do not store your chat messages beyond your session. Conversations are not used to train AI models.
  • We update our safety guidelines as standards evolve.
  • If the AI ever gives you a response that feels harmful or inappropriate, please report it to hello@liamscall.com — your feedback directly improves the tool.

Frequently asked questions

What is Liam's Call?

Liam's Call (liamscall.com) is an AI technology project for caregivers and families facing mental health, addiction, and housing challenges.

Who is Liam's Call for?

Caregivers, family members, and anyone seeking next steps around mental health, addiction, or housing support — wherever they are.

Is Liam's Call free?

Yes. The chat is free to use with no account, waitlist, or co-pay.
