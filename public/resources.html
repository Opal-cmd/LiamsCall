Crisis & Support Resources

Ontario & Canada — all lines are free and confidential unless noted.

9-8-8 Suicide Crisis Helpline National · 24/7

Call or text if you or someone you know is thinking about suicide.

Call or text 9-8-8
ConnexOntario Ontario · 24/7

Free help finding mental health, addiction, and crisis services near you. Phone, chat, text, or email.

1-866-531-2600 connexontario.ca
Kids Help Phone Under 29 · 24/7

Professional counselling for children and youth. Available in over 100 languages.

1-800-668-6868 or text CONNECT to 686868
Health 811 Ontario · 24/7

Speak with a registered nurse about mental health, depression, anxiety, or addiction concerns.

Call 811
Hope for Wellness Helpline Indigenous · 24/7

Immediate counselling and crisis intervention for all Indigenous peoples across Canada.

1-855-242-3310
Emergency

If you or someone is in immediate danger, call 911 or go to your nearest emergency department.

Call 9-1-1

Self-Help & Caregiver Tools

Ontario Caregiver Organization

Free resources, workshops, and peer support groups for caregivers across Ontario.

ontariocaregiver.ca 1-833-227-3778
BounceBack Ontario

Free guided self-help program for mild-to-moderate depression and anxiety. Delivered via phone coaching and workbooks.

bouncebackontario.ca
CAMH: Mental Health & Addiction Info

Canada's leading mental health teaching hospital. Their website has guides on depression, anxiety, grief, and supporting a family member.

camh.ca
211 Ontario

Dial 2-1-1 or visit online to find local social services, housing support, food banks, and community mental health programs near you.

211ontario.ca or dial 2-1-1
