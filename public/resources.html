Crisis & Support Resources

Canada & United States — lines below are free and confidential unless noted. We point to official directories instead of listing individual shelter or clinic phones that go out of date. For the closest local resources for your situation, start a chat with Liam's Call AI — support is anonymous and no account is required.

Canada

9-8-8 Suicide Crisis Helpline Canada · 24/7

Call or text if you or someone you know is thinking about suicide. Bilingual support via 988.ca.

Call or text 9-8-8 988.ca
ConnexOntario Ontario · 24/7

Free help finding mental health, addiction, detox, and crisis services anywhere in Ontario. Phone, chat, text, or email.

1-866-531-2600 connexontario.ca
Kids Help Phone Under 29 · 24/7

Professional counselling for children and youth across Canada. Available in over 100 languages.

1-800-668-6868 or text CONNECT to 686868
Health 811 Ontario · 24/7

Speak with a registered nurse about mental health, depression, anxiety, or addiction concerns.

Call 811
Hope for Wellness Helpline Indigenous · 24/7

Immediate counselling and crisis intervention for all Indigenous peoples across Canada.

1-855-242-3310
211 Canada Canada · Local services

Dial 2-1-1 or search online for local housing support, food banks, and community programs. Ontario visitors can use 211ontario.ca.

211.ca 211ontario.ca
Toronto Shelter Central Intake Toronto · Shelter

Looking for a Toronto shelter tonight or soon? Call Central Intake — do not rely on random clinic lists from search results. From within Toronto you can also dial 311.

416-338-4766 1-877-338-4766 311 in Toronto
Ontario Caregiver Organization Ontario · Caregivers

Free resources, workshops, and peer support groups for caregivers across Ontario.

ontariocaregiver.ca 1-833-227-3778
BounceBack Ontario Ontario · Self-help

Free guided self-help program for mild-to-moderate depression and anxiety. Delivered via phone coaching and workbooks.

bouncebackontario.ca
CAMH: Mental Health & Addiction Info Canada

Guides on depression, anxiety, grief, and supporting a family member from Canada's leading mental health teaching hospital.

camh.ca
Emergency Canada

If you or someone is in immediate danger, call 911 or go to your nearest emergency department.

United States

988 Suicide & Crisis Lifeline United States · 24/7

Free support for suicidal crisis, mental health crisis, or substance-use crisis — for the person in crisis or someone worried about a loved one.

Call or text 988 988lifeline.org
SAMHSA National Helpline United States · 24/7

Free, confidential treatment referral and information for mental health and/or substance-use disorders. Not itself a crisis line.

1-800-662-HELP samhsa.gov
FindTreatment.gov United States · Treatment locator

Official SAMHSA locator for mental health and substance-use treatment near you — the U.S. parallel to calling a provincial intake line.

findtreatment.gov
211 United States United States · Local services

Dial 2-1-1 or search online for shelters, food, housing, and local community services in your area.

211.org or dial 2-1-1
NAMI HelpLine United States · Caregivers

Information, resource referrals, and support for people living with mental health conditions and their families.

1-800-950-NAMI nami.org/help
Emergency United States

If you or someone is in immediate danger, call 911 or go to your nearest emergency department.

Frequently asked questions

How do I find a Toronto shelter tonight?

Call Toronto Shelter Central Intake at 416-338-4766 or 1-877-338-4766, or dial 311 in Toronto. You can also use 211 Ontario (211ontario.ca). Liam's Call does not list individual shelter phones because vacancies change constantly.

How do I find Ontario detox or addiction treatment near me?

Call ConnexOntario at 1-866-531-2600 or visit connexontario.ca. They match people to current withdrawal management and addiction treatment openings across Ontario.

How do I find mental health or addiction treatment in the United States?

Call the SAMHSA National Helpline at 1-800-662-HELP or use the official locator at findtreatment.gov. For local shelters and community services, dial 211 or visit 211.org.

Is Liam's Call a crisis line or medical service?

No. Liam's Call is a free informational AI chat for caregivers and families. It is not a medical professional, therapist, or emergency service. In a crisis, call or text 9-8-8 (Canada or the U.S.), or call 9-1-1 for emergencies.

Do I need an account to use Liam's Call?

No. Liam's Call is free and does not require an account or login.
