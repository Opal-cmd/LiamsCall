Blog desk

Review drafts, make small edits, then publish. No coding needed.

View public blog

This is the private blog desk for reviewing and publishing posts. Ask the site owner for the blog desk password if you do not have it yet.

Site owner setup (one time): put BLOG_ADMIN_PASSWORD=your-password in the project .env file, restart the server, then share that password with whoever reviews drafts.

Simple 3-step flow
  1. Open a draft under Needs review
  2. Read the preview. Change the title or text if something feels off
  3. Click Approve & publish — it goes live on the blog right away

Use Live on site later if you need to take a post down and turn it back into a draft.
